What Are Call and Put Options?

Call Option: Gives the right (not obligation) to buy a stock at a strike price before expiration. Call price increases if stock goes up.

Put Option: Gives the right (not obligation) to sell a stock at a strike price before expiration. Put price increases if stock goes down.
